Oil Prices, Historic

The novel coronavirus outbreak has wreaked havoc across the world and has severely impacted the global economy. On Monday, in a historic drop, the oil prices plunged below USD zero. Amid the novel coronavirus pandemic, the demand for energy has collapsed. Traders are refraining from buying crude oil with nowhere to store it, and hence, oil prices plummeted to negative USD 35.20 on Monday.
